About Mini Noticeboard

📋 What is Mini Noticeboard?

Mini Noticeboard is a simple yet powerful web application designed for sharing campus updates and important notices with the community. Whether you need to announce events, share important deadlines, or post general updates, Mini Noticeboard makes it easy.

✨ Key Features
  • Easy Notice Creation: Post notices with just a title, content, and author name
  • Pin Important Notices: Mark urgent notices to keep them at the top
  • Edit Anytime: Update your notices even after posting
  • Delete Option: Remove notices you no longer need
  • Clean Interface: Beautiful, user-friendly design
  • Timestamp Tracking: See when notices were posted and updated
🚀 How It Works
  1. Click "New Notice" to create a post
  2. Enter your notice title and content
  3. Provide your name or organization
  4. Optionally pin the notice if it's important
  5. Submit and your notice goes live immediately
🎓 Technical Stack

Backend: Django (Python web framework)
Database: SQLite
Frontend: Bootstrap 5, HTML5, CSS3
Architecture: Model-View-Template (MVT) pattern

💡 Why This Project?

Mini Noticeboard demonstrates core Django concepts in a practical application:

  • URL routing and view functions
  • Template rendering with context
  • Database models and migrations
  • Form handling and validation
  • Django ORM queries
  • Admin interface
📝 Quick Tips
  • Use the admin panel (admin/) for managing notices with more control
  • Pinned notices automatically float to the top
  • You can edit and update notices after posting
  • Share notices with your community for better engagement
  • Keep notices updated for accurate information
